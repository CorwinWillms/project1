Bri Sindelar

Bri Sindelar is a Richmond, Virginia based singer, dancer, actor,
and musician. She has extensive training in all elements of music
theatre and is a hardworking, dedicated member of the
Richmond theatre community. She will be graduating from Glen
Allen High School in 2023 and plans pursue a BFA in musical
theatre in college.
